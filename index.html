Augmented Reality Demo

See an astronaut in your world!

How to Use

  1. Open this webpage on your smartphone (iPhone or Android).
  2. You'll see a 3D model of the astronaut spinning above.
  3. Tap the AR icon in the bottom-right corner of the astronaut view. (It looks like a 3D cube or square).
  4. Your camera will open. Point it at a flat, well-lit surface (like the floor or a desk).
  5. Move your phone around slowly until the astronaut appears. You can then walk around it!
